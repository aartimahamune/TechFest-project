TechFiesta

Ignite Innovation. Inspire Ideas. Involve the Future.

TechFiesta is the ultimate celebration of technology, creativity, and innovation. Join us for thrilling competitions, hands-on workshops, inspiring talks, and a showcase of the brightest minds from across the nation.

OUR EVENTS

HACKATHON
Hackathon is an exciting 24-hour coding marathon where creative minds come together to build innovative tech solutions. Whether you're a beginner or a pro, it's your chance to brainstorm, collaborate, and bring your ideas to life — fast!
Online Quiz
Put your tech knowledge to the test! From coding concepts to the latest innovations, this fast-paced quiz game is designed for tech enthusiasts of all levels. Compete solo or with friends to see who can score the highest and earn the title of Tech Whiz!
Web Page Design
Unleash your creativity and coding skills in this design battle! Craft visually stunning, user-friendly web pages that impress both judges and users. Whether you're a front-end pro or a passionate beginner, this is your chance to shine and showcase your UI/UX brilliance!
